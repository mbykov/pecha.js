about Pecha.js

 

Pecha, དཔེ་ཆ་ - (cholok: pe.cʰa, Wylie: dpe cha), means Tibetan traditional book.

hermeneutics, not linguistics:

Morpheus family of analyzers, in particular Pecha.js, a.k.a. Morpheus-for-Tibetan are not linguistic programs. Their goal is to help in reading and understanding the ancient text. In order not to introduce a modern way of thinking (that is, what we think and how we used to think in our days), Pecha.js is being developed and will be developed taking into account books of the ancient grammarians, starting with T. Sambhota, and not in accordance with modern linguistic theories.

local dictionaries for a text:

Pecha.js is integrated with platform for creation of multilingual books Diglossa.js http://diglossa.org/diglossa.js , and allows you to create local dictionary for a text or corpus of the texts.

phonetic transliteration Cholok:

Pecha.js has a built-in phonetic transliteration module Cholok. Just press Shift, or if you prefer traditional cumulative reading, Shift and then Control - http://diglossa.org/tibetan/cholok

Note, Cholok shows not the modern linguistic's ideas of transliteration, and not the actual pronunciation of native speakers. But pronunciation, as it described by ancient grammarians.
