to / from CSV file:

importing from CSV file:

first line in your-file.csv should contains three-letter code, according ISO 639-3 codes: https://iso639-3.sil.org/code_tables/639/data, i.e. "tib,deu", "tib,eng,rus"

exporting to CSV file:

click "toCSV" button on arrange local dicts page. CSV file will be placed in your home dir
